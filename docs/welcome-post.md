# 👋 Start here — Welcome to the AWS SAA-C03 Community Question Bank

> Paste this into a new **GitHub Discussion** (category: *Announcements*), then **pin** it. Replace `<OWNER>/<REPO>` with your repo path.

---

Welcome! This is a **free, open, community-built** bank of scenario-based practice questions for the **AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate (SAA-C03)** exam. Every question comes with a detailed explanation of *why* the right answer is right, *why each wrong option fails*, and a diagram to make the architecture stick.

We're here to **learn together and help each other pass** — beginners very welcome. 🎉

## 🚀 How to use the bank
1. Browse [`questions/`](../../tree/main/questions) — questions are grouped by the four exam domains.
2. Read a scenario and **try to answer before revealing** — each answer sits in a collapsible block.
3. Study the explanation and diagram. If a fact looks outdated, that's a great thing to flag (see below).

## 🙌 How to contribute (no experience needed)
- 💡 **Have a question idea?** Open an issue → **"Add / propose a question"**. You can just paste your idea in the form — a maintainer will help shape it.
- 🐞 **Spotted a wrong answer or an outdated AWS fact?** Open an issue → **"Report an error"** with a source link.
- 🔧 **Want to add it yourself?** Read [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](../../blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md), copy [`QUESTION_TEMPLATE.md`](../../blob/main/QUESTION_TEMPLATE.md), and open a Pull Request.
- 🌱 **New to open source?** Look for issues labelled **`good first issue`** — they're scoped to be beginner-friendly.

## ✅ What makes a great question here (the one-minute version)
- A realistic scenario with real constraints.
- A **decisive qualifier** in the question (MOST cost-effective, LEAST operational overhead, HIGHEST availability…).
- Distractors that each fail for **one precise reason**.
- A **Mermaid diagram** (no ASCII).
- Time-sensitive facts flagged with a source.

## 💬 Where to talk
- **Q&A** — ask study questions in Discussions.
- **Issues** — for adding questions or reporting errors.
- **Show & tell** — share what helped you, mnemonics, or your exam-day notes.

## 🤝 Be kind
We follow a [Code of Conduct](../../blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Assume good faith, help newcomers, keep it constructive.
